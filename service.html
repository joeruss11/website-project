Process

01

Choose Your Plan.

Select the strategy that fits your business—Free, Pro Growth, or Enterprise. Whether you're looking for basic visibility or maximum exposure with higher commissions, our flexible plans are designed to suit your needs and help you connect with city explorers.
02
(Design)

Create Your Profile.

Enter your business details, upload images, and write your own profile. Our automated system makes it quick and easy to set up, ensuring you have full control over how your business is presented.

Once your profile is complete, your business will be added to the map and integrated into our audio tours. Customers can now discover, engage, and visit your location effortlessly.
